# Operators & Aliases

War Selection uses the same Lua operators as native Lua.

| Lua | Note |
|:---:|:-----------------------:|
| `and` | And operator
| `or` | Or operator
| `not` | Not operator
| `~=` | Not equal to operator
| `--[[ ]]` | Multi-line comment operator
| `--` | Single-line comment operator
